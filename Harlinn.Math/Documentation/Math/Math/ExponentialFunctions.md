# Exponential functions

[Optimized basic math functions](Readme.md)

- [Exp](Exp.md) Calculates $e$ raised to the argument ($e^{x}$).
- [Exp2](Exp2.md) Calculates $2$ raised to the argument ($2^{x}$).
- [ExpM1](ExpM1.md) Calculates $e$ raised to the argument and subtracts one ($e^{x}-1$).
- [Log](Log.md) Calculates the natural (base-$e$) logarithm of the argument.
- [Log10](Log10.md) Calculates the base-10 logarithm of the argument.
- [Log2](Log2.md) Calculates the base-2 logarithm of the argument.
- [Log1P](Log1P.md) Calculates the natural (base-$e$) logarithm of the argument plus one.
